FULFILLING THE 5C'S OF SILLIMAN UNIVERSITY

For its brand of a holistic approach to education, Silliman centers the learning and development of its students in the 5Cs, five venues for learning outside the Classroom, the first “C”. The other Cs are the Church, Court, Culture, and Community. This website was created by Neil Arthur B.Divinagracia in an attempt to showcase all the skills, values and lessons he accumulated from each event attended.
